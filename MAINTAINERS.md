## Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge pull requests in their area of ownership
- Triage issues and apply labels
- Participate in release decisions
- Uphold the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Represent the project in external community processes where relevant

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have made significant and sustained contributions (code, documentation, community) may be nominated as maintainers by an existing maintainer. The process is described in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
